Param Tevar

Founder & CEO | Strategy, Technology & Business Design

Param Tevar is a multidisciplinary strategy and product leader with over 20 years of cross-functional experience spanning marketing strategy, brand architecture, enterprise systems, fintech platforms, AI/ML-enabled products, and business design. His work sits at the intersection of structured strategic thinking and scalable technology ecosystems, with a consistent focus on sustainable enterprise value creation.

With deep exposure to software and data-driven environments, Param has led AI-powered and analytics-centric product initiatives, owned product roadmaps, contributed to enterprise system architecture discussions, and guided cross-functional engineering teams toward measurable business impact. His approach integrates technical architecture, regulatory awareness, and commercial strategy—ensuring that innovation is both operationally executable and economically defensible.

At KNOVOSIS, Param leads with a systems-oriented philosophy. He focuses on building institutional-grade operating models, scalable digital platforms, and product-led growth frameworks designed for resilience, governance alignment, and long-term competitive advantage rather than short-term momentum.
